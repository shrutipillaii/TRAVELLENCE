Research on Competitors

Insights from Primary and Secondary Data

A. GAPS Model

The GAPS Model helps identify and close gaps between customer expectations and perceptions.

  • Gap 1: Knowledge Gap – Misunderstanding customer expectations. Travellence uses surveys, reviews, and AI-driven sentiment analysis to reveal traveler preferences, resulting in deeper customer insights and tailored strategies.
  • Gap 2: Design Gap – Poor translation of insights into service design. Personalized itineraries, AI trip planners, and sustainability options make each trip unique and customer-centric.
  • Gap 3: Delivery Gap – Inconsistent service delivery. Skilled consultants, 24/7 support, and CRM-based tracking ensure flawless operations.
  • Gap 4: Communication Gap – Mismatch between promises and real service. Transparent updates, honest pricing, and real-time notifications build credibility and trust.

B. SERVQUAL Model

Reliability – “We deliver what we promise.” Accurate bookings, verified partners, and timely service ensure dependability.

Assurance – “We inspire confidence.” Certified experts, secure payments, and trusted travel partners boost customer trust.

Tangibles – “We look as good as we serve.” A sleek app and modern website reflect Travellence’s premium brand.

Empathy – “We care personally.” Dedicated managers and flexible policies help build strong relationships.

Responsiveness – “We act fast.” 24/7 live support and quick resolutions keep travelers confident and supported.

C. Brand Differentiators

  • Superior Digital Experience: A smooth, unified platform for booking, payments, and tracking — no third-party redirects.
  • Transparency & Trust: No hidden fees or confusing fine print. Clear prices and policies build confidence.
  • Human Touch + Smart Tech: AI efficiency blended with human warmth — certified advisors and 24/7 support.
